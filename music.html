Campfire Sessions Album of the Week

In each episode, the boys take turns nominating an "Album of the Week". In the following episode, they briefly discuss and rate the album based on five criteria:

  • Album Cover (0-5)
  • Title (0-5)
  • Vocals (0-10)
  • Instrumental (0-10)
  • Cohesiveness (0-10)
  • Enjoyment (0-10)

These ratings add up to a potential total of 50 points. Below, you can find all the previous albums the boys have discussed, along with the current "King of the Hill" (highest scoring album to date) to the right.
