Campfire Sessions Album of the Week

In each episode, the boys each nominate their own album of the week. In addition to choosing their own albums, they take turns every week spinning a wheel to nominate an album based on a specific catergory they land on. Examples of this include choosing a specific genre or picking an album that starts with a specific letter. In the following episode, they discuss and rate the albums based on five criteria:

  • Album Cover (0-5)
  • Title (0-5)
  • Vocals (0-10)
  • Instrumental (0-10)
  • Cohesiveness (0-10)
  • Enjoyment (0-10)

These ratings add up to a potential total of 50 points. After they rate the albums out of 50, they take the average of their 3 scores to get a final score.
