What Is Campfire Sessions?

Our podcast covers a wide array of topics with one goal in mind:
feeling like you're sitting around a campfire with some of your best friends. We don’t follow a strict format, which enhances the casual vibe of our show. Each episode might feature discussions on music, movies, pop culture, current events, and what's happening in our lives. We also enjoy playing games, whether they’re ones we create ourselves or classic favorites.

Meet the Hosts

Campfire Sessions Podcast is hosted by Kyle, Cody, and Josh. Kyle and Cody, childhood friends, launched the podcast in 2023. Josh, Kyle's older brother, joined as a permanent host on episode 3.

Our Global Reach

Our podcast has reached listeners across five continents including the United States, Germany, Mexico, Iceland, and Australia.

Our Logo

The Campfire Sessions Podcast logo was hand drawn digitally by Josh and Kyle's mom. Thanks to her personal love of drawing and writing, she was able to draw inspiration from the Campfire Sessions name itself to create a fun and unique logo to represent our brand.
